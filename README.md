# Career-Loop
Career-Loop is an all-in-one academic and skill-development platform designed to support students in both their studies and personal growth. The website combines social, analytical, and learning tools to create a supportive digital environment where students can stay motivated and achieve their goals.
One of the core features of Career-Loop is the community and friend system, where students can connect with peers who share similar academic interests. Within these communities, users can exchange notes, discuss topics, and motivate each other, creating a collaborative learning atmosphere.
The platform also includes a practical attendance predictor. This tool helps students track their attendance and calculates how many classes they must attend to meet minimum attendance requirements. It also shows how many additional classes are needed if attendance is low, allowing students to plan better.
Another key feature is the AI Resume Analyzer, which evaluates a student’s resume and provides feedback on its suitability for specific companies or roles. It also suggests improvements, helping students build stronger, more competitive resumes.
The Skill Exchanger feature allows students to teach subjects or skills they are good at. Other students can enroll in these sessions for a minimal fee, creating a peer-to-peer learning system where both the teacher and learner benefit.
Overall, Career-Loop is designed as a comprehensive platform that combines social interaction, academic tools, and skill-based opportunities to help students grow academically and professionally.
